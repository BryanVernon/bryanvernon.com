About Me

Hi, I'm Bryan Vernon. Born in Argentina, I’ve grown up with a multicultural background and am fluent in both English and Spanish. Early on, my curiosity for the world of biology steered me into Computational Biology, blending my passion for science with technology. This fascination opened doors to many fields, from cloud computing to data analysis, all of which continue to drive my professional journey.

While my love for sports saw me playing Division I rugby for the Texas Longhorns, it also sparked an interest in sports data analysis. I’m currently working on curating a comprehensive sports data database and even placed in the top 25% of a college basketball data science competition.

Outside of sports and tech, I enjoy diving into hands-on projects, always eager to learn more. From automating processes to building robust cloud solutions, I’m constantly looking for new ways to challenge myself.
